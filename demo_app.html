Key Insights from the Data
🏷️
Brand Dominates Pricing
The car brand explains 56.6% of the price variance — the single strongest predictor. Luxury brands (BMW, Audi, Mercedes) command 5–8× premiums over economy cars.
📉
Rapid Age Depreciation
Car age accounts for 35.6% of price variance. Depreciation is steepest in years 1–5 (~8% per year), then slows. A 5-year-old car retains only ~60% of its value.
⚡
Electric & Diesel Premium
Electric cars fetch ~30% over comparable petrol models. Diesel commands ~10% premium due to lower running costs. CNG cars see slight discount vs petrol.
📋
Owner History Penalty
Each change of ownership reduces price ~15%. First-owner cars are strongly preferred by buyers. Fourth+ owner cars may be priced at 45–55% of first-owner equivalents.
